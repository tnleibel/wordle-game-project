Wordle

The Rules
-The goal of Wordle is to guess a secret five-letter word.

-You have six tries to guess the secret word.

-Each time you submit a guess, the letter tiles in that row will flip and change color!

-Grey letters are not in the secret word.

-Yellow letters are in the secret word, but are in the wrong position within it.

-Green letters are both in the secret word and in the correct position.

-Five green letters means you've correctly guessed the secret word!
